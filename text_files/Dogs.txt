Dogs are known as man's best friend for a reason. They are loyal, loving, and always happy to see their owners. Dogs come in all shapes and sizes, from tiny Chihuahuas to massive Great Danes. They are also incredibly intelligent and can be trained to do all sorts of amazing things, from guiding the blind to assisting police officers. If you are thinking about getting a dog, make sure you are ready for the responsibility. Dogs require a lot of time and attention, as well as proper training and exercise.
